# Storyline Flowchart - Complete Reference

## Overview

The storyline begins with acquiring the Armored Case from the crashed plane on Woods. Your first major decision determines your path:

- **Keep the Case** ‚Üí Opens case yourself, then choose to work with Kerman or not
- **Give to Prapor** ‚Üí Prapor opens case, additional Lightkeeper steps, then choose Kerman

6. **Crafting**: Perform 50H craft at Intel Center

## üìã PHASE 3: THE ENDINGS (Summary)

The final extraction process is the same for all paths:

> Head to Shoreline between 22:00 and 04:00, talk to the Intercom at the Tower, Swipe your Keycard/Note, approach the Terminal (without Weapon in Hand) and start your attempt to escape.

### 1. Survivor Ending (Prapor Path)
- **Item Required**: Note for the Soldiers
- **If Escape Fails**: Buy new Note for 5M ‚ÇΩ at Prapor (Limit: 2 per Reset)
- **Unlocks**: Survivor Ending Badge/Achievement

### 2. Savior Ending (Fence/Kerman Path)
- **Item Required**: Crafted Keycard
- **If Escape Fails**: Craft the Keycard again (11H Craft time, requires new Blank RFID Card)
- **Unlocks**: Savior Ending Badge/Achievement

### 3. Debtor Ending (Lightkeeper Path)
- **Item Required**: Lightkeeper's Keycard
- **If Escape Fails**: Barter new keycard from Lightkeeper for 1 Blue Folder
- **Unlocks**: Debtor Ending Badge/Achievement

### 4. Fallen Ending (Prapor Hard Mode - WIP)
- **Requirements**: Path still being documented
- **Unlocks**: Fallen Ending Badge/Achievement
