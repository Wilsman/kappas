# 1.0 Storyline Quests - Detailed Reference

This document contains detailed information about how to trigger each storyline quest in Escape from Tarkov 1.0.

**Source:** Community research (VeryBadSCAV and others)  
**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

---

## 1. Tour

- **Trigger:** Starting quest - automatically unlocked at the beginning of the storyline
- **Icon:** 1.png

---

## 2. Falling Skies

- **Trigger:** Go to the plane in Woods / if you keep on doing Tour, it will auto unlock
- **Best Method:** Keep doing Tour until completing Mechanic's quest, then go to the broken plane in Woods
- **Icon:** 2.png
- **⚠️ Known Issues:**
  - Quest is rumored to be bugged
  - Some reports say standard accounts need 0.2 Prapor Reputation to progress
  - Quest actually requires "Reach Prapor LVL 2" which may cause softlock for some players

---

## 3. Batya

- **Trigger Methods:**
  - **Option 1 - Visit Grenade Launcher Locations:**
    - Stronghold Customs
    - Shoreline near tank behind Sanatorium
    - Reserve Dome
  - **Option 2 - Find BEAR Patch:**
    - Woods: At the convoy below dead BEAR body
    - Customs: Radio Tower next to new gas station, up the hill in small cabin below pillow
- **Icon:** 3.png

---

## 4. The Unheard

- **Trigger:** Activated by picking up a Note on the table in Streets
- **Icon:** 4.png

---

## 5. Blue Fire

- **Trigger Locations (any one):**
  - Woods med camp: Inside a **Green container** taped to a white drawer
  - Interchange: Flyer in the new area
  - Interchange: Big Terragroup area behind old co-op extract, med tent/bunker
- **Icon:** 5.png

---

## 6. They Are Already Here

- **Trigger Methods (any one):**
  - Kill cultist
  - Loot dorms marked room
  - Get a note next to the cultist circle in abandoned village
  - Fisherman island on Shoreline where the green box is
- **Icon:** 6.png

---

## 7. Accidental Witness

- **Trigger:** Check the car between Customs dorms
- **Icon:** 7.png

---

## 8. The Labyrinth

- **Trigger:** Going into the access tunnel in Shoreline Resort Basement
- **Requirements:** Knossos key is needed
- **Reference:** https://escapefromtarkov.fandom.com/wiki/Knossos_LLC_facility_key
- **Icon:** 8.png

---

## 9. The Ticket

- **Trigger:** Earned automatically after completing "Falling Skies"
- **Icon:** 9.png

---

## Notes

- Some triggers may have multiple methods - players only need to complete one
- Information is community-sourced and may be updated as more details emerge
- Quest order matters - some quests unlock others (e.g., Falling Skies → The Ticket)
